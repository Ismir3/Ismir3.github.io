About Me

Hi there! So let me tell/write you my story... As already mentioned, I have a degree in Economics and am an aspiring web and app developer. But... lets start from the very beginning... I was born and raised in Zenica, Bosnia (back then ex Yugoslavia) in 03.02.1984 (damn I'm old :P)... so, feel free to buy me something on that date, I wouldn't mind at all :P ... Anyway, even in kindergarten, I was kind of an artist, drawing and painting like nobody else there, and continued to be and do that in elementary school as well, where I collected some awards for best drawings too, appearing in news papers and... stuff... I was good in mathematics too (but I am not very good at memorising lectures)... ok, so later, I finished high school (for an architect), but instead of proceeding in that direction, I enrolled and graduated from the Faculty of Economics. After that, I volunteered in the Tax Office for a year and, while searching for a job in my profession, worked all kinds of part-time jobs, from which I would single out work with children in the basketball club (for which I started to work on the website, but I did not finish because of ended relationships with them). That was a fun job, because I love to work/play with children (maybe because of that child in me), and not once have I heard from someone that I would be a great father one day (I do hope so...). I love animals too (maybe I'm destined to work in ZOO one day.. who knows...).

In addition to these jobs, I've completed a lot of courses, live and online, like english courses, basics in web and app development, SolidWorks 3D modeling, Photo editing, some game development... to round up, I am well versed in the basics of the aformentioned fields, but I'm definitely interested in learning some more...

Anyway, due to lack of work/jobs in the profession, I completed the training for "a responsible person" in the field of protection/security, and for a year now I work as a Security Guard (currently in local Agency - Sector Security).

I will continue to work on this web site/blog, but for now, (pretty) please follow me on Facebook and/or LinkedIn and tell your family and friends about it (or else...). If you want to get in touch, please click on the contact link in the navigation bar. (or here, if you're too lazy to get up there again :)) And, preferably, offer me a better job, maybe :)
